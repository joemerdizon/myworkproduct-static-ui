About

I am a single mother raising one child and am currently employed as a nurse assistant. Growing up on a farm, I developed a deep affection for animals. My life revolved around tending to crops, harvesting produce, and selling them at our local farmers' market for years. Living with my grandparents, I became intimately familiar with caregiving. They battled chronic illnesses, and we were responsible for providing home care until their passing. Now, I am driven to earn money to further my nursing career and fulfill my ambition of becoming a registered nurse (RN).

What I Offer

  • Farming and animal care expertise to assist others in agricultural pursuits.
  • Guide crop cultivation, harvesting, and selling at farmers' markets.
  • Assist in-home caregiving for individuals with chronic illnesses.
  • Mentor aspiring nurses
  • Pet care

Services Offered

  • Assist and coach in vegetable garden creation
  • Elder Care: Chronic illness
  • Sales Coaching: Farmers' markets
