About

I am resilient and determined mother of six whose life took a dramatic turn when the steel mill and my taxi business collapsed, plunging my family into poverty. Undeterred by adversity, I harnessed my industrious spirit to guide my children toward a better future. While caring for my partially paralyzed husband, battling severe hypertension and colon cancer, I worked tirelessly in multiple jobs to lift my family out of poverty.

Through sheer determination, I navigated the challenges of financial aid, scholarships, and part-time work, ensuring my children had access to higher education. Their talents and hard work flourished, propelling them into successful careers as doctors, nurses, allied health professionals, an attorney, and even entrepreneurs. Witnessing their achievements fills me with immense pride and validates my sacrifices.

What I Offer

  • Inspire and motivate other families by sharing her story of resilience and determination.
  • Provide guidance and resources on accessing financial aid, scholarships, and part-time work for children's education.
  • Offer practical advice on managing multiple jobs while balancing family responsibilities.
  • Connect families with local community resources, including food banks, charities, and educational initiatives.
  • Mentor and support individuals caring for loved ones with health challenges.
